How it works
Four steps, every single night
Nyay Saathi runs quietly in the background of VerdictAI's case management, watching for anything that changes.
1

Fetch eCourts data

Every tracked case is checked nightly against the public eCourts case status record.

2

Compare to last known state

Any change in hearing date, stage, or new order is detected automatically.

3

AI plain-language translation

Legal English is rewritten into warm, clear text in the family's own language.

4

WhatsApp delivery

The family receives the update directly — no app to install, no portal to check.
